Features

Everything your household needs

Built around real grocery habits — fast to log, easy to share, clear to understand.

One-tap logging
Pick a shop, enter the amount, hit save. The whole flow takes under 10 seconds — designed for the checkout queue.
Household sharing
Create a household and invite members with a 6-letter code. Everyone's transactions are pooled automatically.
Budget tracking
Set a monthly household budget and watch it in real time. A visual progress bar keeps everyone honest.
Spend categories
Tag each transaction — Grocery, Meat, Health, Clothing. Add your own custom categories at any time.
Real-time sync
Powered by Supabase. The moment one member logs a shop, everyone else's totals update — no refresh needed.
Activity history
Every transaction is timestamped and stored. Review your household's full spending history at any point.
